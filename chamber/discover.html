Quick Facts:

The City was incorporated in December of 1997.

Saratoga Springs is only partially developed and it is expected that the build-out population of the City will be over 120,000 residents.

According to Saratoga Springs Website

More Events:

Miss Saratoga Springs Scholarship Pageant

The Miss Saratoga Springs Pageant is a program designed to help young women in Saratoga Springs serve their community and gain scholarship money for higher education.

Spring Festival

Come welcome Spring with an Easter egg Hunt, pancake breakfast, and other fun activities.

Splash Days

Saratoga Splash Days is the city celebration for Saratoga Springs. It is held the 2nd full weekend of June.

Summer Monday Fundays

Celebrate the beautiful summer weather with our Monday night Food Truck Rally, free concerts and other fun, family friendly activities.

Train Rides

The Utah Live Steamers offer rides at Shay Park on Saturday mornings through the summer. The run schedule will be posted in May.

Get To The River

The Get to the River Festival is a month-long series of community events along the Wasatch Front sponsored by the Jordan River Commission, These events are centered on the Jordan River and Jordan River Parkway in September.

Fall Festival

Celebrate the crisp air and crackling leaves at the annual Fall Festival. Enjoy games, train rides, food trucks and more.

Veteran's Flag Ceremony

Join us as we honor those who served our country and read the names of the new additions to the Veterans Monument.

Celebrate in Saratoga

From Musical Concerts, a visit from Santa, to Christmas Light shows and more, there is sure to be something for every member of the family to celebrate the holidays in Saratoga Springs.

Adaptive Accommodations

Saratoga Springs staff want all residents to be able to spend time together and enjoy our great, growing community.
